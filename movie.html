Mr. Robot - Review

Rating: 8.5/10
Genre: Drama, Thriller
Creator: Sam Esmail
Starring: Rami Malek
Years: 2015-2019
Seasons: 4

Review

Mr. Robot is an amazing TV series about a hacker named Elliot who works as a cybersecurity engineer during the day but hacks bad companies at night.

The show is really good because:

  • Rami Malek's acting is excellent
  • The hacking scenes look realistic
  • The story is very interesting
  • It talks about important social issues

Some things that might be difficult:

  • The story can be confusing sometimes
  • It has serious and dark themes
  • You need to pay attention to understand it

Final Opinion

Mr. Robot is a great show for people who like psychological thrillers and technology. It's one of the best TV series of recent years and I recommend watching it if you enjoy complex stories.

Recommendation: Definitely worth watching!
